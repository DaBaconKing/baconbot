BaconBot Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BaconBot handles data when you use the bot on Discord.

1. What We Collect

BaconBot may log non-personal metadata such as command usage, timestamps, server IDs, and user IDs. No message content or personal information is stored.

2. Why We Collect It

Data is used to monitor bot performance, detect abuse, and improve functionality. We do not use data for advertising or profiling.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose any collected data to third parties. All logs are kept private and used solely for bot maintenance.

4. User Rights

You may request deletion of any stored data related to your Discord ID by contacting the bot owner. We will comply promptly.

5. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of BaconBot implies acceptance of the latest version.
